Slowing Progression and Lowering Risk of Acute Exacerbations

Now that you understand IPF progression and why it’s so serious, it’s time to do something about it.


While there is no cure for IPF, there are treatment options available that have been shown to slow IPF progression. The sooner you begin treatment, the sooner you may start slowing IPF progression.
